Connecticut Carbon Monoxide Affidavit and Property Disclosure Credit

Property Disclosure Credit: Required for all transactions except forclosures or estate sales.

Smoke Detector/Carbon Monoxide Affidavit: Only required on homes built before OCtober 1st, 2005.

  • PAR Team member only needs to confirm if the credit information is listed on the purchase agreement itself.
Before Adding Credits

Check DocViewer to see if the Property Disclosure and the Smoke Detector and Carbon Monoxide Affidavit have been uploaded.

  • if the documents have been provided with the Purchase agreement, no further action is needed (client not eligible for credit).

Rocket Mortgage Loans: In the Item Tracking Menu, press Insert to add a Proeprty ACD Conflict Action Needed-3640 tracking item with comments tating that the Realtor must provide the documentation or confirm in the client will receive the credit.

  • If the client will receive the credit, the SPS will email the assigned PAR team member.
  • Once the email is received, look at adding Carbon Monoxide Affidavit and Property Disclosure below.
  • We CAN NOT give the credit until a response has been received.

Rocket Pro TPO Loans: Call the partner to confrim that the client will receive the credit. Verbal communication is sufficient to apply or remove the credit.

Adding Carbon Monoxide Affidavit and Property Disclosure to AMP
  • Step 1: From the Purchase Agreement Review screen, press Tab on Prorations to access the Tax and Proration Summary screen.
  • Step 2: Press Intert to add the credit. Type Y for yes when answering Would the client be reimbursed?.
  • Step 3: Press Enter on the Carbon Monoxide prorations and enter the total number of credits. When completed press Home to exit. When Please complete data range entry for this manual prorations press OK, as there are no date ranges for this credit.

Due Diligence Fees

Enter the prorations screen located on the bottom right of AMP and press insert to add a credit to the buyer. We want to ensure that we choose due diligence fee and "Should the client be reimbursed" to state Yes.

Enter Due Diligence fee into AMP and press enter to bypass the "Please complete data range entry for this manual proration" message.

Due Dilligence Fee Comment(s)
  • Rocket Mortgage Loans: in the comments, use the following template to update the underwriter on the fee:
    "Hello team! Per page [], in section [] of the OA, there is a Due Diligence Fee for $[]. This has been entered into the prorations screen. If this needs to be removed and added as an EMD, please remove it from the prorations screen prior to adding the amount to AQ or reach out to PAR to have fee removed and added as an EMD. Thank you!""
  • Rocket Pro TPO Loans: in the commenets, use the following template tu update the underwriter on the fee:
    "Hello team! Per page [], in section [], there is a Due Diligence Fee for $[]. This has been entered into the prorations screen. Please confirm with the partner if this is to be used as an additional EMD. If so, please remove the due diligence fee from prorations and update AQ with the amount. Thank you!"
